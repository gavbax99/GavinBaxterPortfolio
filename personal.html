A HELPFUL MAGIC: THE GATHERING TOOL
Magic: the Gathering is a strategic player-vs-player card game that has been around since the early 1990s. While playing a game of Magic it is crucial to plan your strategy around the hidden cards that your opponent may have in their hand. You never know what they could be, but with this useful tool a user can input the opponent's avaliable resources and they will be returned a list of cards they could portentially have based on those resources.

Built with a simple JS api and sorting algorithm.
