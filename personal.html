Play Around Me Calculator
A HELPFUL MAGIC: THE GATHERING TOOL
Magic: the Gathering is a strategic player-vs-player card game that has been around since the early 1990s. While playing a game of Magic it is crucial to plan your strategy around the hidden cards that your opponent may have in their hand. You never know what they could be, but with this useful tool a user can input the opponent's available resources and they will be returned a list of cards they could potentially have based on those resources.

Built with a simple JavaScript API and sorting algorithm. Note: as of 6/2019 scryfall.com has changed their image URL naming conventions which in turn has rendered my API broken. Fixes to come.
