Ultimate Luchador
11/2018
A React browser game with mobile playability in mind. I was the lead graphic designer, game designer, and developer for this project, personally committing nearly 80,000 lines of code over the course of four weeks.

Ultimate Luchador is a text-based, player-vs-player RPG game. You begin your journey as a rookie with the hopes of raising your Fame level to become the Ultimate Luchador. You have three abilities that change and get more powerful as you level up your Luchador by fighting against other players with a similar Fame level.

Ultimate Luchador is built on a SERN stack - MySQL, Express, React, and Node. We utilized Redux for easier access to our game data.
