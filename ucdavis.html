Ultimate Luchador
REACT.JS WEB BROWSER GAME
For our final project I chose to design and build a React.JS browser game with mobile playability in mind. I was the lead graphic designer, game designer, and developer for this project, personally committing nearly 80,000 lines of code over the course of four weeks.

Ultimate Luchador is a text-based, player-vs-player RPG game. You begin your journey as a rookie with the hopes of raising your Fame level to become the Ultimate Luchador. You have three abilities that change and get more powerful as you level up your Luchador by fighting against other players with a similar Fame level. Winning raises your Fame and losing lowers it, so choose your opponents wisely!

Ultimate Luchador is built on a SERN stack - MySQL, Express, React.JS, and Node.JS. We utilized Firebase for account creation and authentication.

Orion Trail
HMTL WEB BROWSER GAME
You're the captain of a intergalactic star ship. After plummeting through an uncharted wormhole you and your crew find yourselves stranded in space with the the goal of staying alive and finding your way back home to the Orion galaxy.

Inspired by the classic Oregon Trail, Orion Trail is a choose-your-own-adventure style game where every decision you make effects your progress and ultimately your fate.

I was the lead developer and game designer on this project. I developed a system that used five data attributes attached to each of our buttons that handled both the changing of stats and page navigation through Handlebars.JS. Because of this design we only required two AJAX calls to run our game as well allowing for easy modulation for future additions.

Build on a Node.JS, Express, and MySQL foundation.
