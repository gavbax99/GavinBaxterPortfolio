Website Redesign
BIG BROTHER'S REDESIGNED HOME PAGE
Kenny, the owner of Big Brother Comics, had grown beyond the bland and bloated website he had been previously using. He wanted a website that was cleaner, faster, more on-brand, and most importantly one that was responsive for mobile devices.

Redesigned from the ground up I used his red, white, and black color pallet along with the theme of the store - George Orwell's 1984 'Big Brother' - to create a website that better reflected his brand.

Kenny wanted the ability to post bulletins of upcoming events to his website. I implemented a simple and secure user portal for him and his employees to post (or hide) bulletins that would post to a MySQL database through Node.JS and Express.
