11/2018 - 4/2019
The Electric & Gas Industries Assciation (EGIA) is a nonprofit organization committed to helping contractors achieve success in their business through a variety of services, tools, and member resources. Located in Sacramento, CA serving thousands of members nationwide since 1966.

I worked as the Front-End Developer for EGIA first as a freelance contractor, then as a full time employee. My job was to create responsive websites for both EGIA and our associates using HTML, CSS, JS, and WordPress as a page management system. I worked closely with the Graphic Designer to create beautiful and easy-to-use websites for our clients.

EGIA SUMMER EVENT PAGE
EGIA hosts a large annual conference in Las Vegas to bring together industry experts and our members for the "Ultimate Educational Experience for Contractors."
